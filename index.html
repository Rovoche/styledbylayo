Logistics & Services

Worldwide Reach

Based in Lagos, Nigeria, SBL ships bespoke garment crates and luxury ready-to-wear lines globally. We guarantee perfect fitment through our digital custom measurement protocol.

I

Secured Global Crates

Our evening and wedding gowns are packed into cushioned wooden crates containing custom form hangers, ensuring seamless shipping via DHLExpress to North America, London, and worldwide destinations.

II

Virtual Diagnostics

Online consultation portals and video calls guide global clients through our detailed 35-millimeter anatomical calibration measurement protocol, securing absolute fitment prior to cutting patterns.

III

Lagos Atelier

Experience highly personalized appointments, custom fabric selections, and tambour frame walkthroughs by scheduling private fittings with our master couturiers.
